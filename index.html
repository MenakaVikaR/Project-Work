Are you a designer looking to source high quality fabric?

With our decades of experience in the textile industry, we now supply fabrics tailor-made to your needs, specifications and occasion. Our handloom unit consists of over 200 looms spread across all the major silk production centres of Tamil Nadu, especially in Arani, Kanchipuram and Thirucherai. Right from Kanchipuram Silks and Tussar weaves to Breezy Organzas and Banarasi Brocades, the options we provide are endless. Our family of artisans also include an in-house team of specialists in the fields of printing, embroidery and stonework. They come with an abundance of experience and have a keen eye for design and detailing, which helps our creative catalogue to grow.

Shefali Khanna x Sundari Silks

It was a delightful experience for us as a brand to be a part of The 5th Walking Hand-in-Hand event, a yearly fashion showcase conducted by the CDS Art Foundation, Ahmedabad. We collaborated with renowned fashion designer Shefali Khanna who features the timeless Kanchipuram weaves in a brand new avatar, by weaving together a melodious harmony of two contrasting worlds of classic and contemporary.

“The challenge was to rethink Kanchipuram sarees and fabricate creations that would appeal to a global fashion-forward audience. The focus was on versatility, finesse and integrity without diluting its legacy. The showcase is to make people pause, rethink and adopt Indian handloom textiles.”
